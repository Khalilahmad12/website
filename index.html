How it works

Once you've selected a vehicle that meets your needs and fits within your budget, to drive it away, we require an initial payment covering the first four weeks and completion of our hire purchase agreement. After this, no further payments are due until week 5, when regular payments will begin. All listed vehicle prices include the appropriate road fund licence. Payments should be made via standing order from your bank or building society, with options for weekly, fortnightly, four-weekly, or monthly payments.

Most vehicles are available for same-day collection after viewing, as they are typically prepared in advance.

At any point after 36 weeks, you have two options:

a) Swap your current vehicle for another and start fresh, without concerns about part exchange values or deposits — subject to a small fee of £150.

b) Continue with your payments until the vehicle is fully paid.

All vehicles come with a 12-month MOT (unless stated otherwise), are fully serviced, and include a 3-month return-to-base warranty covering major mechanical faults for your peace of mind.

This scheme offers unparalleled flexibility compared to traditional car finance options, which often involve credit checks, long-term commitments, and penalties for early termination.

**Required Documentation:**

  • Two forms of ID dated within the last two months showing your home address (preferably utility bills)
  • Your last two payslips or proof of income
  • A bank statement from the account used for payments
  • Your driving licence
  • Proof of fully comprehensive insurance cover
